Frequently Asked Questions

How to purchase an item on Veltrix

Products can be purchased through the product section, and only if you have registered an account with Veltrix that you would be able to buy a product.

How do i find specific Gaming products?

Veltrix is a unique commerce website where gamers can find all types of gaming prodcuts. To find what you want, head to the search bar for a quick search, or select one of our product sections to find a range of items that you are looking for.

What are the shipping costs for products on Veltrix?

We provide 3 different types of shipping, a standard delivery for 4.99 GBP, express delivery for 9.99 GBP, and a next day delivery of 14.99

How can I contact support?

To contact support you can head to the Contact Us section, in which you can fill out your name, email, query title and message.

How can I request a refund?

If you would like to refund a product, go to previous orders, and you will come across the Request Refund page of the chosen product, where you can submit a refund along with a message to why.

Do i need an account to purchase a product?

To purchase a product at Veltrix, you would need to register an account and then you would be able to add a product to the basket and head to checkout. Unregistered users can view products but they are unable to add the product to the basket without an account.

How does wishlist work?

If you are browsing a product and unable to purchase it at the moment, you can select the 'Add to wishlist' button which will add the product to your wishlist, where you can find again later in the wishlist section.
